| Ticket | Approach | Constitution | Trade-offs |

| Ticket | Approach | Constitution | Trade-offs |

**Best**: EXCAL1-5 — Named variables (`topLeft`, `topRight`) express geometric intent better than `points[0]`. The `** 2` simplification is more idiomatic than `(x/w) * (x/w)`.
